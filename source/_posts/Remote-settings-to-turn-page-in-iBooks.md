You can use a Bluetooth remote (like a presentation clicker) to turn pages in Apple Books on iPhone and iPad — handy for reading while your phone is on a stand or across the room.

## How it works

Apple Books responds to Bluetooth keyboard arrow keys and media remote buttons. A Bluetooth presentation clicker typically sends these signals, making it compatible out of the box.
