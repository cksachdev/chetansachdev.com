QuickTime Player on macOS doesn't show a visible pause button during recording — but there is a keyboard shortcut for it.

## Pause / Resume shortcut

Press `Space` while a QuickTime recording is in progress to pause or resume it.

For audio recordings, you can also click directly on the recording waveform in the toolbar.
